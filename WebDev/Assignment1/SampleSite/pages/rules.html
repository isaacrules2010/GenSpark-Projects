True Criticals

Whenever a creature rolls a value on an attack roll that is higher than the target's AC by 5 or more, then that creature rolls damage as normal, then adds their max damage (without modifiers)

Deadly Criticals

Whenever a creature rolls a natural 20 on an attack roll, that creature forgoes all damage rolls and instead does double the maximum possible damage (without modifiers)
